Personal Passions

I have too many passions to be able to include everything here. Science and technology are probably at the top of the list. In my free time, I can’t read enough about physics. Gravitational time dilation and relativity are probably my all-time favorite physics subjects. In my opinion, better funding and education for science and math should be at the forefront of any talk about primary education. I’m also very passionate about animal rights -- a cause that I feel is critical for the future of humanity. As is evident by the fact that I have a food blog of my favorite recipes, I thoroughly enjoy cooking. While my wife and I have been vegetarian for some time, I am more than happy to discuss any type of food with anyone! Food is one of the great equalizers; everyone needs to eat, and everyone enjoys good food. Pies and candies are my favorites to cook. Candy making is more science than art (unlike some cooking) and appeals to me a lot more. If you want to talk food or have any suggestions for recipes, feel free to reach out to me! Foreign languages fascinate me. I tend to pick up little snippets of languages like a sponge and have more recently decided to use that skill to become fluent in several languages. Currently, I am at a conversational / intermediate level in Spanish, and I am working towards fluency in Croatian. One of the great things about learning foreign languages is that they not only open new opportunities, but the act of learning greatly improves other brain functions. My current track for foreign language learning is this: Croatian (which has the added benefit of effectively also teaching you Bosnian and Serbian); regain fluency in Spanish; then fluency in German. After my goals are met, I am excited for the future. After that, the future is wide open. If you have any suggestions, I would be more than happy to hear them!
